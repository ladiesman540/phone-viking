How It Works

The dispatch system in 6 steps. Click "Configure" on any step to jump to its settings.

1
Customer Calls
After-hours call comes in to your Vapi phone number
2
AI Collects Info
Voice agent asks the caller about their emergency
3
Job Created
System creates a case, posts to Slack, checks for duplicates
4
Rule Matched
Routing rules decide which contacts to call based on issue, area, time
5
Tech Dispatched
SMS + AI call sent to techs in escalation order until someone accepts
6
Customer Notified
Customer gets a callback confirming their tech is on the way
